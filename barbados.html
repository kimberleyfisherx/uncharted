Where to Stay

Sandy Lane

Sandy Lane, located on Barbados' prestigious West Coast, is a renowned luxury resort that epitomizes Caribbean opulence. With its luxurious accommodations, including stunning rooms, suites, and villas, guests are treated to a world-class experience. The resort is home to three championship golf courses, including the iconic Green Monkey Course, designed by Tom Fazio. Indulgence awaits at the award-winning spa, offering a range of treatments to rejuvenate the mind and body. Dining options abound, with exquisite cuisine prepared by talented chefs. The resort's private beach invites guests to relax or partake in thrilling water sports. Above all, Sandy Lane's impeccable service ensures that every guest receives personalized attention and a truly unforgettable stay in paradise.

Coral Reef Club

Nestled within lush tropical gardens on Barbados' sought-after West Coast, Coral Reef Club is a charming boutique hotel that epitomizes timeless luxury. With its exquisite accommodations blending classic colonial style and modern comforts, guests are enveloped in elegance. Serenity awaits in the resort's beautiful gardens and on the pristine beach just steps away. Indulge in rejuvenating spa treatments, savor culinary delights at the two exceptional restaurants, and experience personalized service from the attentive staff. Leisure facilities, including pools and tennis courts, cater to active pursuits, while complimentary watersports invite adventure. At Coral Reef Club, guests discover a haven of tranquility where natural beauty and warm hospitality create an unforgettable escape in the Caribbean.

Where to eat and drink

Oistins Fish Fry

Immerse yourself in the vibrant and lively atmosphere of Oistins Fish Fry, a beloved cultural event on the beautiful island of Barbados. Located on the South Coast, Oistins comes alive every Friday night with an explosion of flavors, music, and dancing. This authentic Bajan experience is a must-visit for food enthusiasts and those seeking a taste of local culture. Indulge in an array of freshly grilled fish and seafood, prepared with Bajan spices and traditional recipes. From mouthwatering flying fish to savory lobster, the culinary offerings are sure to satisfy every palate. As you savor the delicious fare, you'll be serenaded by the rhythms of live music, including calypso, reggae, and soca. Join in the festivities, dance to infectious beats, and feel the vibrant energy that permeates the air. Oistins Fish Fry is not just a culinary delight but a cultural immersion, where locals and visitors alike come together to celebrate the rich heritage of Barbados.

The Rum Vault

If you're a rum enthusiast, don't miss The Rum Vault, a hidden gem tucked within the Colony Club Hotel. This intimate and exclusive rum bar boasts an impressive selection of over 150 rums from around the world. Knowledgeable staff will guide you through a rum tasting experience like no other.

Nishi Restaurant

Nestled in a historic chattel house in Holetown, Nishi Restaurant is a hidden gem offering a fusion of Japanese and Caribbean flavors. The talented chef combines local ingredients with Asian influences to create unique and delectable dishes. The intimate setting and personalized service make for a memorable dining experience.

Things to do

Animal Flower Cave

Located in the northernmost point of Barbados, the Animal Flower Cave is a fascinating natural wonder. Venture into the limestone cave to witness its striking formations and stunning ocean views. During low tide, you can even take a dip in the crystal-clear pools inside the cave.

Hunte's Gardens

Discover the enchanting Hunte's Gardens, a hidden oasis nestled in the heart of Barbados. This picturesque garden showcases a stunning collection of tropical plants, towering palms, and vibrant blooms. Take a leisurely stroll through the winding paths and immerse yourself in the beauty and tranquility of nature.
