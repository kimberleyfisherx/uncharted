Blackpool

Our complete guide to Blackpool

Blackpool, a vibrant coastal town on the northwest coast of England, is a beloved destination known for its iconic attractions, lively entertainment, and nostalgic seaside charm. Whether you're seeking a fun-filled family vacation or a nostalgic trip down memory lane, Blackpool offers a plethora of experiences to suit every taste. In this complete guide, we'll explore the top attractions, recommend three exceptional hotels, and highlight the must-visit restaurants and bars in this lively seaside resort.

Where to Stay

The Big Blue Hotel

Located adjacent to Blackpool Pleasure Beach, The Big Blue Hotel offers a luxurious and contemporary accommodation option. With stylish rooms, exceptional service, and modern amenities, this hotel provides a comfortable retreat after a day of exploration. Indulge in delicious cuisine at their on-site restaurant, or unwind with a cocktail at the trendy bar.

The Imperial Hotel

For a touch of grandeur, The Imperial Hotel is a historic landmark that combines Victorian elegance with modern comforts. Overlooking the picturesque North Promenade, this luxurious hotel features spacious rooms, a serene spa, and a delightful afternoon tea experience. The stunning sea views and impeccable service make for a memorable stay.

The Carousel Hotel

Situated on the quieter South Promenade, The Carousel Hotel offers a cozy and welcoming atmosphere. The comfortable rooms, friendly staff, and panoramic views of the Irish Sea ensure a pleasant stay. The hotel's on-site restaurant serves a variety of delicious dishes, including locally sourced seafood.

Where to eat and drink

The Beach House Bistro

Located on the seafront, The Beach House Bistro offers a relaxed and inviting dining experience. Enjoy freshly caught seafood, hearty British classics, and international dishes made with locally sourced ingredients. The restaurant's warm and friendly ambiance, coupled with stunning sea views, creates the perfect setting for a memorable meal.

Abingdon Street Market

Abingdon Street Market, located in the heart of Blackpool, has recently undergone a remarkable transformation. After an extensive refurbishment, it is now reopening as an exciting and vibrant food hall that is sure to delight food enthusiasts of all kinds. The market proudly showcases street food offerings from six local independent businesses, ensuring a diverse range of culinary options to satisfy every palate. Whether you're a fussy eater, someone who struggles to decide what to eat, a dedicated meat lover, a vegan, or fall somewhere in between, this food hall has something for everyone. The variety of vendors ensures that you'll find an array of flavors, cuisines, and dietary preferences all under one roof. You can explore a plethora of tantalizing dishes, from international delicacies to comforting local favorites. In addition to the delightful food offerings, Abingdon Street Market also features a coffee shop, perfect for those in need of a caffeine boost, a cocktail bar where you can unwind with creative and refreshing drinks, and a craft beer lovers bar for beer enthusiasts to indulge in a wide selection of craft brews. With its inviting ambiance, diverse culinary options, and a range of beverages to complement your meal, Abingdon Street Market is set to become a vibrant hub for food lovers in Blackpool. So, gather your friends, bring your appetite, and prepare to embark on a culinary adventure like no other at this newly reopened food hall.

West Coast Rock Blackpool

West Coast Rock Cafe is not only a renowned live music venue but also a fantastic restaurant that promises an unforgettable dining experience in Blackpool. Located along the iconic West Coast Promenade, this rock-themed establishment combines great food, vibrant ambiance, and a love for music. Step inside West Coast Rock Cafe, and you'll be greeted by a captivating rock 'n' roll atmosphere. The walls are adorned with music memorabilia, classic album covers, and iconic artwork that pay homage to rock legends. The restaurant's interior design sets the stage for an immersive dining experience that will transport you into the world of rock music. When it comes to the menu, West Coast Rock Cafe offers a delectable range of dishes to satisfy every appetite. Sink your teeth into mouthwatering burgers, savor succulent steaks, or indulge in their signature rock-inspired dishes. From hearty comfort food to vegetarian and vegan options, there's something for everyone to enjoy. The emphasis is on quality, using fresh and locally sourced ingredients to create flavorsome and satisfying meals. Beyond the fantastic food, West Coast Rock Cafe also boasts a fully stocked bar where you can find an impressive selection of drinks. From craft beers and specialty cocktails to an extensive range of spirits, you can find the perfect beverage to accompany your meal or to enjoy during the lively music performances. Whether you're looking for a memorable dining experience, a night of live music, or a combination of both, West Coast Rock Cafe delivers on all fronts. With its rock 'n' roll ambiance, delicious food, and vibrant atmosphere, it's the perfect spot for music enthusiasts, food lovers, and anyone seeking a unique and entertaining experience in Blackpool.

Things to do

The Blackpool Tower

No visit to Blackpool is complete without a trip to the Blackpool Tower. Ascend to the top of this historic landmark for panoramic views of the town and coastline. Explore the attractions within the tower, including the stunning Ballroom, the Tower Circus, and the Tower Eye observation deck. For an extra thrill, step onto the glass SkyWalk, allowing you to gaze down at the bustling streets below.

Blackpool Pleasure Beach

Prepare for an adrenaline-filled adventure at Blackpool Pleasure Beach, one of the UK's most iconic amusement parks. Experience exhilarating rides such as the famous "Big One" roller coaster, enjoy family-friendly attractions, and indulge in traditional fairground treats. With its thrilling rides, live entertainment, and charming vintage atmosphere, Blackpool Pleasure Beach guarantees a day of fun for visitors of all ages.
