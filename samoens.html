Samoens

Our complete guide to Samoens

Nestled in the heart of the French Alps, Samoëns is a picturesque village that offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, thrilling outdoor activities, and exquisite culinary experiences. Whether you're an avid skier or simply seeking a serene mountain getaway, Samoëns has something for everyone. In this complete guide, we'll uncover three exceptional hotels, three must-visit restaurants and bars, and two exciting things to do, including skiing, in this enchanting alpine destination.

Where to Stay

Hôtel Neige et Roc

Nestled in a peaceful location, Hôtel Neige et Roc is a charming family-run hotel that exudes warmth and Alpine hospitality. With comfortable rooms, stunning mountain views, and a range of amenities including a spa and wellness center, this hotel provides a relaxing retreat after a day of outdoor adventures. The on-site restaurant offers delicious regional cuisine, combining traditional flavors with a modern twist.

Hôtel Le Gai Soleil

Situated just a short distance from the Grand Massif Express cable car, Hôtel Le Gai Soleil is a cozy mountain retreat that combines rustic charm with modern comforts. The tastefully decorated rooms provide a cozy ambiance, and the hotel's wellness facilities, including a sauna and hot tub, offer relaxation and rejuvenation. The friendly staff ensures a personalized experience, and the hotel's restaurant serves delectable Savoyard specialties.

Le Chalet d'en Hô

For a touch of luxury, look no further than Le Chalet d'en Hô. This boutique hotel features elegant rooms adorned with Alpine-inspired decor, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains. Indulge in the hotel's spa and wellness facilities, including a swimming pool and hot tub, or savor gourmet cuisine at their refined on-site restaurant. With impeccable service and attention to detail, Le Chalet d'en Hô promises a memorable stay.

Where to eat and drink

La Table de Fifine

Nestled in a historic farmhouse, La Table de Fifine delights guests with its rustic ambiance and mouthwatering traditional Savoyard dishes. Using locally sourced ingredients, this cozy restaurant serves up hearty specialties like fondue, raclette, and tartiflette. The warm and friendly atmosphere makes for an unforgettable dining experience.

L'Atelier de Jacky

For a gastronomic adventure, head to L'Atelier de Jacky. This Michelin-starred restaurant combines exquisite flavors, innovative techniques, and artistic presentations. With a focus on seasonal ingredients and creative dishes, the culinary creations here are truly a feast for the senses. Allow the knowledgeable staff to guide you through an unforgettable tasting menu.

La Louisiane

After a day on the slopes, unwind with a visit to La Louisiane, a lively bar and restaurant located in the heart of Samoëns. Enjoy a vibrant après-ski atmosphere, indulge in tasty cocktails, and sample their selection of local beers and wines. The friendly staff and live music performances create a convivial ambiance that's perfect for socialising.

Things to do

Skiing in the Grand Massif

Samoëns is part of the vast Grand Massif ski area, offering over 265 kilometers of slopes and breathtaking panoramic views. Hit the slopes and experience world-class skiing or snowboarding on a variety of terrain suitable for all skill levels. With its well-groomed slopes and efficient lift system, the Grand Massif promises an unforgettable winter sports adventure.

Explore Samoëns Village

Take a leisurely stroll through the picturesque village of Samoëns and soak up its charming atmosphere. Admire the well-preserved medieval architecture, browse the local shops for artisanal products and souvenirs, and visit the historic landmark of Samoëns Church. The village's authentic charm and tranquil ambiance make it a delight to explore.
