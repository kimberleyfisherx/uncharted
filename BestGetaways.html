Best Getaways

Our guide to the best getaways

In a world where popular tourist destinations often dominate the travel scene, it can be a challenge to find hidden gems that offer a unique and authentic experience. However, fear not, as we've scoured the globe to bring you a curated list of our favorite off-the-beaten-path getaways. These destinations offer a refreshing break from the typical holiday packages and provide a glimpse into lesser-known treasures. From secluded islands with pristine beaches to quaint villages tucked away in picturesque landscapes, these hidden gems promise to ignite your sense of adventure and offer a truly memorable travel experience. So, pack your bags, step off the beaten path, and discover the joy of exploring new and undiscovered places that will leave you with stories to cherish for a lifetime.

Europe destinations

Český Krumlov, Czech Republic

Tucked away in the southern part of the country, this UNESCO World Heritage site is a picturesque town straight out of a fairytale. With its well-preserved medieval architecture, winding cobblestone streets, and the meandering Vltava River, Český Krumlov offers a charming and romantic escape.

Sintra, Portugal

Just a short train ride from bustling Lisbon, Sintra is a magical town known for its enchanting palaces, lush forests, and mystical atmosphere. Explore the whimsical Pena Palace, wander through the mystical Quinta da Regaleira, and soak in the breathtaking views from the Moorish Castle

Kotor, Montenegro

Nestled in the Bay of Kotor, this medieval town is a hidden gem along the Adriatic coast. With its stunning fortified walls, narrow alleyways, and captivating views of the bay, Kotor is a haven for history lovers and nature enthusiasts alike

Asia destinations

Hoi An, Vietnam

This charming town on the central coast of Vietnam is renowned for its well-preserved ancient architecture, lantern-lit streets, and vibrant local markets. Explore the UNESCO World Heritage Old Town, take a boat ride along the Thu Bon River, and savor the delicious local cuisine.

Gili Islands, Indonesia

Tucked away off the coast of Lombok, the Gili Islands offer a tranquil escape from the bustling tourist hubs of Bali. With no motorized vehicles allowed, these three islands boast pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters perfect for snorkeling and diving, and a laid-back island atmosphere.

Nara, Japan

Just a short distance from Kyoto and Osaka, Nara is a hidden gem that exudes ancient charm. It is home to some of Japan's oldest and largest temples, serene gardens, and, most notably, its friendly population of free-roaming deer in Nara Park.

Africa destinations

Chefchaouen, Morocco

Known as the "Blue City," Chefchaouen is nestled in the Rif Mountains of Morocco. This picturesque town is famous for its blue-painted streets and buildings, creating a unique and mesmerizing atmosphere. Explore the narrow winding alleys, browse the local markets, and soak in the stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

Lamu Island, Kenya

Located on the Kenyan coast, Lamu Island is a tranquil and idyllic retreat. This UNESCO World Heritage site is known for its Swahili architecture, sandy beaches, and rich cultural heritage. Explore the narrow streets of Lamu Town, visit historical sites, and experience the laid-back island lifestyle

Zanzibar Archipelago, Tanzania

Off the coast of Tanzania, the Zanzibar Archipelago consists of several islands, each offering its own unique charm. From the historical Stone Town with its spice markets and Arab-influenced architecture to the pristine beaches and coral reefs of Mnemba Island, Zanzibar Archipelago is a paradise for nature lovers and culture enthusiasts alike.
